Workshops

Team Phoenix offers various workshops for schools and college students. Till date the team has educated more than 3000 students in Goa. Team has experienced seniors who have worked and achieved expertise in the specific domains. Hence its worth to learn and understand topics taught by them in a very interesting and interactive manner. These workshops are specifically designed to get a complete idea about the topics covered with hands-on sessions for everything.

Arduino Workshops

This is an introductory workshop for kick start to programming and using a microcontroller called Arduino. As an outcome of this workshop, one is capable to code and interface various sensors with Arduino and undertake mini projects with Arduino.

Drone Workshop

With technological enhancement drones are becoming too common as a hobby, final year projects, and also used by professional companies for photography, analysis, research etc. With this workshop one is able to understand complete details of all components, assembly, software and working of the overall drone from scratch.

Internet of Things

Since the smart way of living has begun life has changed drastically with everything controlled over the internet. As such for any enthusiast it is very important to understand its workings for it. This workshop enables you to understand and have a complete idea of how IOT works.

Line Follower Robots

Line follower robot is a very interesting robot. The task of this robot is to complete the given track in the fastest possible time. This workshop enables you to learn and make use of the robot with detailed explanations of the logic and sensors used for it. At the end, one can experience their self built robot completing the track.

Combat Robotics

This is most sought after, where one understands the life cycle of an actual robot making. One learns and gets a good idea from planning, designing, components selection,software and analysis, material science, manufacturing, electronics and lot more things used for the robots made for Robowars

The main aim of the Team Phoenix is to excel and evolve in the field of robotics, industrial automation, project development and related sectors by fostering Skills of innovation, research and entrepreneurship
