About Team Phoenix

Team Phoenix is a professional organisation formed in 2015 of skilled, passionate and enthusiastic engineers. Team Phoenix has expertise in the field of Robotic Automations, Industrial Product Development, Professional Training and Technical Event Management.

Aim

To uplift a student into a responsible person having industrial skills, professional management supported by communication skills along with technical know-how hence making a self-reliant individual possessing entrepreneurial abilities in all aspects.

Motives

  1. Delivering out of box solutions for daily life problems
  2. Spreading knowledge gained over years to interested youth
  3. Excelling in team work, collaboration and coordination
  4. Acquiring knowledge and skills to stay up to date with technological development
