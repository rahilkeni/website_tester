Product Description

Automatic Hand Sanitizer

Its covid19 time and taking the necessary safety precautions is of utmost importance. Hence we are here with an Automatic Hand Sanitizer. It's totally contactless and very easy to use. Its dispenses mist hence avoids unnecessary wastage of the liquid. The machine also comes with battery backup of 3-4 days and can be used in remote places or places where power is not available. This wall mountable machine is hence perfect for home, office and industries, for the best price. We provide 1L, 5L, 8L,10L machines.

Hand Held UV Sterilizer

Wondering how to sanitize your clothes, wearable, furniture, rooms and all items which cant be sanitized through liquid sanitizer then this product is a perfect product for you. Sanitize any object within minutes. Being easy to use is also very efficient. This machine is very dynamic in nature and very safe to use.

Corona Oven

As covid19 cases are increasing day by day, we need to make sure we have proper sanitation of every item we use. But we also know it is not possible to sanitize everything using liquid sanitizer hence this corona oven can be used to sanitizer objects like mobile, wallets, documents, paper currencies etc. This product can be perfect for office and industrial purposes.
